About SKITS

SKITS is a non-profit organization aimed at bringing the joy of science for children around the country. Starting as a simple volunteering service for elementary schools, SKITS is now selling science kits for children and schools around the nation.

SKITS Chemistry Camp

Students in grades 1-6 learn all about chemistry at SKITS' in-person chemistry camp! High school students from schools like Whitney and Cerritos collaborate to help kids get hands-on experience with chemistry experiments, letting them understand how and why they happen through fun projects.

Summer STEM Immersion Program

SKITS is continuing its STEM Immersion Program in 2026! It is a math, chess, and coding tutoring program for elementary students that started during the summer of 2020. Teachers will be high-performing high and middle schoolers.
