About SKITS

SKITS is a non-profit organization aimed at bringing the joy of science for children around the country. Starting as a simple volunteering service for elementary schools, SKITS is now selling science kits for children and schools around the nation.

Learn More About Us

Science Kits (SKits) Program

SKITS' in-person Science Kits program brings hands-on STEM experiments to elementary students through hour-long sessions led by passionate volunteers. Students build, test, and explore topics like chemistry, physics, and engineering — all completely free.

Learn More

STEM Immersion Program

SIP is a free, virtual program where middle and high school volunteers teach hour-long STEM classes to elementary students. Over 4–5 weeks, students explore topics ranging from marine biology and coding to chess and engineering — no prior knowledge required.
