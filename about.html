Who Are We?

Skits is a 501(c)(3) Non-Profit Organization, dedicated to educating young kids on everything STEM-related. Started by a group of ambitious and hardworking students at Whitney High School, Skits was created to be an outlet of creativity and help for elementary students.

Meet Our Team

What Have We Done?

When we first started, we created an online tutoring service to guide elementary students online. This was a success, having children leave with bright faces and curious minds! Eager to connect again with these students, we made a hands-on STEM class called “Discovery Lab”, teaching kids basic science topics with fun projects. All in all, SKITS have come a long way from where they started, and we are excited to keep expanding our horizons.
