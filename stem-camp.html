STEM Camp

Sign-ups for our IN-PERSON 2025 Fall STEM Camp are now open!

Spots are fist-come, first-serve.

Sign Up Here!

When and Where will the Camp be Held?

Classes will be held on August 23rd, September 20th, and October 18th, at the Holy Family Church (18708 Clarkdale Ave, Artesia, CA 90701).

Classes will be held from 3-4 PM!

What is our STEM Camp?

Young students will learn about topics like engineering, condensation, pressure changes, and more! Students will be taught by Whitney, Cerritos, and other high school student tutors who excel in the subjects they are teaching and are qualified to teach young children.

If you'd like your child to receive an immersive, engaging, and intellectual STEM lab experience, this is the perfect opportunity for you!

Who Can Sign Up?

Any elementary school student that is located in or near Artesia can sign up to be part of this year's STEM Camp.

If you'd like your child to receive an immersive, engaging, and intellectual STEM lab experience, this is the perfect opportunity for you!
