Meet the Team

The passionate people behind SKITS: Science for Kids

Stephanie Lin

Co-President

Hello! My name is Stephanie Lin and I am a junior at Whitney High School. My favorite STEM subject is biology because I find it interesting to learn about the little things inside organisms that create the world around us. I first started my experience with Skits as a student of their Stem Immersion Program, during the summer of the pandemic, before becoming involved in providing these exciting opportunities for younger students in the community. Now, as president, I am excited to see SKITS grow over the next few years!
