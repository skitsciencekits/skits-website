STEM Immersion Program

Student and volunteer sign-ups for our Spring STEM Immersion Program are open!

Student sign-ups are due January 25 and volunteer sign-ups are due January 18.

Student Sign Up Volunteer Sign Up

What Is the STEM Immersion Program?

The STEM Immersion Program is a program where talented middle and high school students teach math, chess, and coding courses to elementary school students. Each week, students will participate in engaging, interactive sessions led by qualified student teachers who are passionate about STEM and skilled at teaching young learners. Students are welcome to enroll in as many classes as they wish!

If you’re looking for a fun, educational, and inspiring spring experience for your child, this program is a wonderful opportunity.

When will the Program be Held?

Classes will be held online via Google Meet starting Sunday, February 1st. They will be running from 5-6 PM and 6:30-7:30 PM over four weeks.

Course Information

Students and Teachers

  • Any elementary school student can sign up.
  • Teachers are high-achieving and passionate about what subject they teach.
  • They range from middle to high school students.

What Is Taught

  • We have math classes of one class for each grade level.
  • Students will be taught the curriculum they are learning at school, to reinforce the concepts that they have learned
  • There are also also group chess and coding classes in addition to math.

Why Did We Start This Program?

We are passionate about bringing mathematics and STEM to students everywhere. Through the Summer STEM Immersion Program, students can join us to learn different STEM Subjects and have fun all from the comforts of their home.
