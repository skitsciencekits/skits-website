STEM Immersion Program (SIP)

The Online STEM Immersion Program introduces students to a wide variety of STEM topics through free, virtual classes led by passionate middle and high school volunteers.

What Is SIP?

The Online STEM Immersion Program (SIP) is a virtual program designed to introduce students to science, technology, engineering, and math in an engaging and accessible online format. Each SIP runs for 4–5 weeks and consists of hour-long classes held virtually. Classes are led by middle and high school volunteers who teach topics they are passionate about and knowledgeable in.

Students may enroll in as many classes as they would like, and all classes are completely free. Classes focus on building curiosity and exposure rather than prior knowledge, making them accessible to students of all backgrounds.

Classes We've Hosted

Science

  • Oncology, Ecology, Marine Biology, and more

Technology

  • Artificial Intelligence, Coding, Biotechnology, and more

Engineering

  • Engineering Design, CAD, and more

Math & Enrichment

  • Grade 1–6 Math, Logic, Statistics, Chess, Careers in STEM, and more

Who Can Participate?

Students

  • Open to elementary school students (grades 1–6)
  • Only a device with internet access is needed
  • Enroll in as many classes as you'd like — all free

Volunteers

  • Middle and high school students teach subjects they are passionate about
  • Volunteers receive community service hours after the program
  • All volunteers attend a one-hour training before teaching

Volunteer Structure

Each class typically includes:

  • 1 Lead Teacher — teaches the lesson and communicates with parents
  • 1 Teaching Assistant (TA) — helps create materials and fills in if the teacher is absent
  • 1–4 Tutors — help prepare materials and answer students' questions during class

Volunteers collaborate to create slides, interactive activities, and educational games to keep classes engaging.

After the Program

  • All volunteers receive community service hours for preparation and teaching time
  • Each volunteer receives an official SKITS volunteer certificate
  • Feedback forms are sent to parents and volunteers to improve future programs
