Since Summer 2020...

SKITS originated in light of the pandemic, which shut down many learning opportunities for younger kids.

As a response, we at SKITS dedicated ourselves to devise creative and accessible ways to catalyze STEM opportunities for kids. The past summer, our board has worked tirelessly towards this goal, and we are proud to share our accomplishments.

Within the past five years we...

  • Built and launched our official website
  • Officially became a registered Nonprofit Organization
  • Recruited our Board! Check us out here!
  • Overcame obstacles of a Global Pandemic
  • Began Hosting In-Person Classes!
